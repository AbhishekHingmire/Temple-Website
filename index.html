About Us

About Sitaram Maharaj Samadhi Mandir, Khardi.

Shri Sitaram Maharaj Trust, Khardi, is the Administrative body of Sitaram Maharaj's Samadhi Temple and all others temples in this premises, and devoted towards development of Khardi village.

Sitaram Maharaj Trust, Khardi, is the authorised body to control and manage the day-to-day activities at the Shri Sitaram Maharaj Samadhi Temple. It also provides various facilities like Accommodation, Meals (Free), refreshments, and lot more.

Khardi is a secular place where all religions are treated as one & a belief in the power of Faith & Patience is foremost. A place where all heads bow down in prayer, where faith prevails, where hopes are built, where patience pays, and where infinite joy and everlasting contentment abound.

Our Annual Events

PUNYATITHI MOHOTSAV

The punyatithi mohotsav is of 7 days, in which 7 days of different kirtankar peoples serves kirtan seva to the devotees.

RAM NAVMI

Rama Navami is a spring Hindu festival that celebrates the birthday of lord Rama. He is particularly important to the Vaishnavism tradition of Hinduism.

HANUMAN JAYANTI

Hanuman Jayanti is a Hindu religious festival that celebrates the birth of Lord Sri Hanuman, who is immensely venerated throughout India and Nepal.

DATTA JAYANTI

Datta Jayanti, also known as Dattatreya Jayanti, is a Hindu festival, commemorating the birth day celebration of the Hindu god Dattatreya (Datta).

TUKARAM BEEJ

Chants of Tukaram, Tukaram' rented the air as around three lakh devotees thronged the temple town of Dehu on the occasion of Tukaram Beej.

KRUSHNA JANMASHTAMI

Janmashtami is the birth day of Gokul Ashtami, the birth of Krishna.
